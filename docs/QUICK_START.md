# Talastock Quick Start Guide

**Get up and running in 10 minutes!** ⚡

---

## 1. First Login (1 minute)

1. Open Talastock
2. Enter email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

---

## 2. Add Your First Category (1 minute)

1. Go to **Categories**
2. Click **"Add Category"**
3. Type: "Food" (or your category)
4. Click **"Create"**

Repeat for more categories (e.g., Beverages, Household, etc.)

---

## 3. Add Your First Product (2 minutes)

1. Go to **Products**
2. Click **"Add Product"**
3. Fill in:
   - Name: "Sugar 1kg"
   - SKU: "FOD-SUG-001"
   - Category: "Food"
   - Price: ₱75
   - Cost Price: ₱60
   - Initial Stock: 100
   - Low Stock Alert: 10
4. Click **"Add Product"**

**Tip:** Use a consistent SKU format like `CAT-PROD-001`

---

## 4. Record Your First Sale (2 minutes)

### Option A: Using POS (Recommended)

1. Go to **POS**
2. Search for "Sugar"
3. Click the product
4. Click **"Complete Sale"**
5. Select **"Cash"**
6. Enter amount received
7. Click **"Complete Sale"**
8. Done! 🎉

### Option B: Manual Entry

1. Go to **Sales**
2. Click **"Add Sale"**
3. Add products
4. Click **"Complete Sale"**

---

## 5. View Your Dashboard (1 minute)

1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. See your:
   - Total products
   - Revenue
   - Inventory value
   - Sales charts

---

## 6. Generate Your First Report (2 minutes)

1. Go to **Reports**
2. Find **"Sales Report"**
3. Click **"Export (Excel)"**
4. Open the file
5. Review your sales data

---

## 7. Set Up Barcode Scanner (Optional - 5 minutes)

1. Connect USB barcode scanner
2. Go to **POS**
3. Scan a product barcode
4. Product should appear in cart

**Need help?** See `docs/pos/BARCODE_SCANNER_SETUP.md`

---

## Common Tasks

### Add Multiple Products Quickly

1. Go to **Products**
2. Click **"Import"**
3. Download template
4. Fill in Excel
5. Upload file

### Adjust Stock

1. Go to **Inventory**
2. Find product
3. Click **"Adjust Stock"**
4. Enter new quantity
5. Click **"Save"**

### Apply Discount in POS

1. Add products to cart
2. Click **"Add Discount"**
3. Choose type (%, fixed, or Senior/PWD)
4. Enter value
5. Click **"Apply"**

### Process Refund

1. Go to **Sales**
2. Find the sale
3. Click **"Refund"**
4. Select items
5. Click **"Process Refund"**

---

## Tips for Success

✅ **Start Small** - Add 5-10 products first  
✅ **Use Categories** - Organize from day one  
✅ **Set Low Stock Alerts** - Never run out  
✅ **Train Your Team** - Show them the POS  
✅ **Check Reports Weekly** - Track your progress
